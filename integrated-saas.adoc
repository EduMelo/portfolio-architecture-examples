Providing integration with Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) applications, platforms, and services empowers organizations to build and run scalable applications in modern, dynamic environments such as public, private, and hybrid clouds. Red Hat offers a foundation for IT teams to develop and deliver integration with SaaS applications through a combination of integration and process automa-tion technologies.
